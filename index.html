A safety mission statement is a short, carefully-crafted message that communicates the safety culture and goals desired by ownership and management to all employees in the organization. It also outlines the broad strategies used to attain the desired safety culture and goals. A safety mission statement can bring your entire team together as you decide why safety is important to each of you.
